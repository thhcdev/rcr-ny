Frequently Asked Questions

Can cream carpets be used outdoors?

Yes! They're great for dry outdoor events like garden weddings or patio receptions. Just be sure to lay them on a clean, dry surface.

How do I keep a cream carpet clean?

Vacuum gently before use, use a protective underlayer if outdoors, and blot any spills quickly.

Are custom sizes or logo printing available?

Absolutely. We offer fully customizable lengths, widths, and even branding options for corporate or personal events.

Do cream carpets work for themed events?

Definitely. Cream is the ultimate neutral, it goes with anything from gold glam to rustic chic.
