Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use maroon runners outdoors?
Yes! They perform well on solid surfaces. For grass or gravel, use tape or anchoring accessories for best results.

Are they slip-resistant?
Yes, the backing is non-slip, but we suggest additional securing methods for high-traffic or active spaces.

Do you offer custom sizing?
Absolutely. Just tell us your measurements and we’ll cut it to order.

How do I care for my carpet runner?
Vacuum after use, spot-clean if needed, dry thoroughly, then roll the fiber side in. Store it in a dry place to maintain its quality.
