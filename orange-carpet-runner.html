Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use orange carpet runners outside?
Yes! They're suitable for solid outdoor surfaces. Use tape or anchors on uneven ground.

Are the runners slip-resistant?
Yes, they come with non-slip backing. For extra grip during large events, we suggest double-sided tape.

Do you offer custom sizes?
Absolutely. We cut to your specifications for a perfect fit.

How do I clean and store it?
Vacuum, spot clean if necessary, allow to dry, and roll it up fiber-side in. Store in a dry, cool place.
