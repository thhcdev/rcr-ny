Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use silver carpet runners outdoors?
Yes, ideal on solid surfaces. For grass or uneven ground, secure with tape or anchors.

Are these runners slip-resistant?
Yes, with non-slip backing. For high-traffic areas, double-sided tape adds extra grip.

Do you offer custom sizes?
Absolutely, we cut to your specifications for a perfect fit.

How do I clean and store it after use?
Vacuum, spot clean if needed, let dry, then roll with the fiber side inward. Store in a cool, dry place.
