Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use turquoise carpet runners outdoors?
Yes! They work beautifully on solid surfaces. For grass or uneven ground, we recommend securing them with tape or anchors.

Are these runners slip-resistant?
Yes, each runner has a non-slip backing. For extra safety at high-traffic events, add double-sided tape.

Do you offer custom sizes?
Absolutely, we cut to order so your runner fits perfectly.

How do I clean and store it after use?
Vacuum, spot clean if necessary, allow to dry, then roll with the fiber side inward. Store in a cool, dry location.
