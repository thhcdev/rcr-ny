Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use white carpet runners outdoors?
Yes! They’re perfect for smooth surfaces. For grass or uneven ground, secure with tape or anchors.

Are these runners slip-resistant?
Yes, the non-slip backing helps, but for large events we recommend double-sided tape for extra grip.

Do you offer custom sizes?
Absolutely, we cut to order for a perfect fit.

How do I clean and store it after use?
Vacuum, spot clean, let dry, then roll with the fiber side in. Store in a cool, dry place.
