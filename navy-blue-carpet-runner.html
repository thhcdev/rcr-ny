Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use navy blue carpet runners outdoors?
Yes! They're excellent on solid surfaces. For uneven terrain, secure with tape or anchors.

Are these runners slip-resistant?
Yes, all runners include non-slip backing. For high-traffic areas, we recommend using double-sided tape for extra grip.

Do you offer custom sizes?
Absolutely, we cut to order so your carpet fits your space precisely.

How do I clean and store it after use?
Vacuum or spot clean as needed, allow it to dry, then roll it fiber-side in. Store in a dry, cool area.
