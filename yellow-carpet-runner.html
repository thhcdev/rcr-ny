Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use yellow carpet runners outdoors?
Yes! They’re great on solid surfaces. For grass or uneven areas, secure them with tape or anchors.

Are they slip-resistant?
Yes, our runners have non-slip backing, but for extra security, we recommend double-sided tape for busy events.

Do you offer custom sizes?
Absolutely. We cut each carpet to fit your space exactly.

How do I clean and store it?
Vacuum, spot clean if needed, dry completely, then roll with the fiber side inward. Store in a cool, dry place.
