Frequently Asked Questions

Can gold carpets be used outside?
Yes! As long as the surface is clean and dry, gold carpets are perfect for patios, outdoor ceremonies, or garden paths.

How do I clean and care for it?
Vacuum gently before use. If outdoors, use a liner underneath and spot-clean spills quickly.

Can I order custom sizes or get my logo printed?
Absolutely. We offer fully custom lengths, widths, and branded printing to match your event or brand.

Does gold work with all color themes?
Totally. Gold pairs beautifully with black, white, navy, emerald, burgundy, blush—you name it.
