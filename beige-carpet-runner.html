Frequently Asked Questions

Can beige/tan carpets be used outdoors?
They can be used outdoors in dry, fair-weather conditions. Consider covered areas for best longevity.

Will beige carpets show dirt or stains?
Not as much as you’d think! These colors are excellent at hiding light debris and come in stain-resistant options.

Are custom sizes available?
Yes! We offer custom widths, lengths, and even logo printing to meet your exact needs.

How do I clean and store beige carpet runners?
Vacuum regularly, treat spills promptly, and roll the carpet with fibers facing inward for storage in a dry space.
