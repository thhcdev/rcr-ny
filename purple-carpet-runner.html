Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use purple carpet runners outdoors?
Yes! They’re ideal on solid surfaces. On grass or uneven terrain, secure with tape or stakes for best results.

Are these carpets non-slip?
They come with a grippy backing, but for safety at high-traffic events, we recommend double-sided carpet tape.

Do you offer custom sizing?
Absolutely. We cut to your exact measurements for a perfect fit.

How should I clean and store my runner?
Vacuum, spot clean, allow to dry fully, then roll up fiber-side in. Store in a cool, dry space.
