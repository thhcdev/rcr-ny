Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use brown carpet runners outdoors?

Yes, our brown carpets are great for outdoor use in dry weather. Look for our outdoor-friendly materials.

How can I prevent slipping?

Use double-sided carpet tape or non-slip rug pads, especially on hard floors.

Do you offer custom sizes?

Absolutely! We’ll cut your brown carpet runner to fit your exact needs.

How do I clean and store the carpet after use?

Spot clean immediately, air dry thoroughly, then roll up with the fibers facing inward and store in a cool, dry place.
