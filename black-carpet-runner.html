Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use black carpet runners outdoors?
Yes, our carpets can be used outdoors in dry, mild weather conditions. For long-term outdoor use, contact us for specialized options.

How do I prevent the black carpet from slipping?
Use double-sided carpet tape or weighted edges to secure it in place on hard surfaces.

Can I request a custom size or logo?
Absolutely. We specialize in custom sizing and logo printing for both rentals and purchases.

How do I clean and store the black carpet after use?
Gently spot clean stains, allow the carpet to dry, roll with fibers inward, and store in a dry place. Rentals should be returned in original condition.
