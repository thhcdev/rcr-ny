Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use red carpet runners outdoors?

Yes! They work great on solid surfaces. For grassy or uneven areas, we recommend securing them with tape or anchors.

Are these runners slip-resistant?

Yes, our carpets come with non-slip backing, but for high-traffic events, we recommend double-sided tape for added security.

Do you offer custom sizes?
Absolutely. We cut to order so your carpet fits your space perfectly.

How do I clean and store it after use?
Vacuum it, spot clean if needed, let it dry, then roll it with the fiber side inward. Store in a cool, dry place.
