Frequently Asked Questions

Can light blue carpets be used outdoors?
Yes! They're great for dry, outdoor events like beach weddings or garden receptions. Use a clean, flat surface underneath for best results.

How do I keep a light blue carpet clean?
Vacuum gently before use, lay a protective base if outside, and address spills promptly with blotting and light cleaners.

Do light blue carpets work for themed events?
Totally. Whether you're going coastal, pastel, modern, or romantic, light blue adapts beautifully.
